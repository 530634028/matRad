matRad

An open source multi-modality radiation treatment planning sytem

Download this project as a .zip file Download this project as a tar.gz file

matRad is an open source software for radiation treatment planning of intensity-modulated photon, proton, and carbon ion therapy. matRad is developed for educational and research purposes; it is entirely written in MATLAB.

Development team

The development is mainly driven by the research group Optimization Algorithms within the Division of Medical Physics in Radiation Oncology at the German Cancer Research Center in Heidelberg but we also have other researchers contributing to our developments.
